How do I manage club members?

Navigate to the club dashboard and go to the ‚ÄúMembership Requests‚Äù section to approve or reject new members.

Can I schedule events?

Yes, go to the Events section and fill out the form. Events need approval from the Activity Center Admin.

How do I respond to club membership applications?

In the Membership Requests tab of your club dashboard, you can view pending applications and choose to accept or reject them. The applicant will be notified of your decision.

How do I create a poll in the group chat?

Inside your club's group chat, scroll below the message box to create a poll. Polls will appear in real-time and members can vote directly from the chat.

Can I message club members directly?

Yes, use the one-to-one messaging feature under the Chat Rooms dropdown. You can only message members of the same club.

How do I upload club-related documents?

Go to the Club Documents section on your dashboard. There you can upload and manage documents relevant to your club activities.

How can I view feedback or complaints from members?

You can access the Feedback section from your dashboard to view suggestions and anonymous complaints submitted by club members.

How do I reschedule an event?

If you need to reschedule a previously approved event, go to the event details page and submit a reschedule request. It must be approved by the Activity Center Admin.

Can I see who voted in a club election?

No, votes are encrypted and anonymous. You can only see vote counts after admin verification once voting ends.

How do I nominate myself or someone else for elections?

Visit the Elections page and choose a position to nominate yourself for. All nominations are subject to admin approval.

Can I change the language of the dashboard?

Yes, use the language selector on the top right of the page to switch between supported languages. Your selection will persist across pages.
